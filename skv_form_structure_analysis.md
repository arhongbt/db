# Swedish Skatteverket Bouppteckning Blankett (SKV 4600 / SKV 461) - Structure Analysis

## Executive Summary

The Swedish Tax Agency (Skatteverket) bouppteckning blankett (estate inventory form) is used to create an official written record of a deceased person's assets and liabilities at the time of death. The form exists in two versions:
- **SKV 4600**: The fillable/printable form itself
- **SKV 461**: The instruction booklet (Utgåva 8 is the current version as of 2024)

---

## Overall Form Purpose

A bouppteckning is a comprehensive document that must be completed and registered with Skatteverket within three months of death, with registration required within one month after preparation. Without an approved and registered bouppteckning:
- Estate beneficiaries cannot close the deceased's bank accounts
- Securities cannot be transferred
- Property cannot be sold
- Cooperative apartments cannot be transferred

---

## Form Structure and Layout

### Primary Sections by Page:

#### **Page 1 - Deceased Person Information & Estate Administration**
- Deceased person's full name
- Complete address
- Full identification number (personnummer)
- Registration area
- Family law status (civil status)
- Date of death (dödsdatum)
- Citizenship information (if applicable)
- Designation of bouppgivare (estate informant/reporter)
- Designation of förrättningsmän (two external officials who verify the estate)
- Addition of estate participants: If the number of fields for recording all persons called to the estate inventory is insufficient, users can click "Lägg till en person" (Add a person) to expand the form as needed

#### **Page 2 - Continued Estate and Survivor Information**
- Verification of pre-filled information
- Relationship to deceased documentation
- Surviving spouse information (if applicable)
- Additional estate co-owner information

#### **Page 3 - Assets (Tillgångar/Egendom)**
The assets section includes detailed categorization of all property owned by the deceased:

**Real Property (Fastigheter):**
- Land properties
- Buildings
- Residential properties
- Commercial properties
- Property designation information and description

**Bank Accounts and Bonds (Bankmedel och obligationer):**
- Bank account information with account numbers
- Deposit accounts
- Bonds and fixed-income securities
- Account balance values as of date of death

**Securities (Värdepapper):**
- Shares (aktier) - Stockholm Stock Exchange listed and unlisted
- Stock market securities
- Funds
- Stock options
- Valuation requirements: Market-traded securities valued at 75% of listed value; Stockholm Stock Exchange shares valued at 75% of lowest asking price on date of death
- Certificate of value requirements for proper identification

**Personal Property (Lösegendom):**
- Ordinary household items (möbler och hushållsföremål)
- Cash and liquid assets
- Vehicles (personbilar)
- Boats (båtar)
- Jewelry
- Art and collectibles
- Other valuables
- Insurance without beneficiary designations

#### **Page 4 - Liabilities (Skulder)**
The liabilities section documents all debts:

**Types of Debts:**
- Mortgage debts (pantlånade skulder)
- Personal debts
- Tax debts (if applicable)
- Outstanding loans
- Other creditor claims
- Funeral costs (prioritized in payment order)
- Costs for the bouppteckning itself (prioritized in payment order)

**Liability Summary:**
- Total debts calculation
- Debt priority order documentation (priority for funeral and bouppteckning costs)

#### **Page 7+ - Additional Information, Declarations, and Signatures**
- Date of the proceedings (förrättningsdatum)
- Confirmation statements (attestation language)
- Signatures of bouppgivare
- Signatures of förrättningsmän (two external officials)
- Final summary calculations
- Estate administration status

## Key Form Fields and Requirements

### Mandatory Information:
- Complete deceased person identification
- Complete list of all assets with descriptions and values
- Complete list of all liabilities with amounts
- Identification of persons with inheritance rights
- Proper valuations according to Skatteverket requirements
- Documentation requirements (certificates of value for securities, property descriptions, etc.)

### Field Categories:

| Category | Contents | Requirements |
|----------|----------|--------------|
| **Deceased ID** | Name, personnummer, address, dödsdatum | Complete and accurate |
| **Estate Participants** | Bouppgivare, förrättningsmän, co-owners | Must designate properly |
| **Real Property** | Property description, designation numbers, valuations | Must include legal property descriptions |
| **Bank Accounts** | Account numbers, institution, balances | Balance as of date of death |
| **Securities** | Type, quantity, valuation method, certificates | 75% valuation rule for market-traded |
| **Personal Property** | Description, category, valuation | Fair market value estimation |
| **Debts** | Creditor, amount, due date | All debts must be listed |
| **Calculations** | Total assets, total debts, net estate value | Arithmetic verification required |

## Formatting and Compliance Requirements

### Skatteverket Mandates:

1. **Fillable PDF Format**: Form can be completed as fillable PDF (requires Adobe Reader or compatible software) or printed and handwritten

2. **Page Order**: Pages must remain in specified order as set by Skatteverket

3. **Completeness Requirement**: Cover page includes a checklist to verify:
   - All required information has been completed
   - All necessary documents have been attached
   - Form is ready for Skatteverket registration

4. **Document Attachments**:
   - Certificates of value for securities (värdebesked)
   - Property registration documents (lagfartsbeslut)
   - Bank statements (kontoutdrag)
   - Asset valuations and descriptions

5. **Signature and Authentication**:
   - Must be signed by bouppgivare
   - Must be verified by two förrättningsmän (external officials)
   - Signatures authenticate the truthfulness of information

6. **Naming Convention**:
   - Official form designation: SKV 4600 for blank form
   - Official instruction designation: SKV 461 (multiple editions/utgåvor)
   - Current instruction version: Utgåva 8 (2024)
   - Previous versions: Utgåva 3-7 (different dates)

---

## Data Organization

The form follows a logical chronological and categorical organization:

1. **Who** (Estate participants and deceased identification)
2. **What** (All assets by category)
3. **How Much** (Valuations and calculations)
4. **What Owed** (Liabilities and debts)
5. **When** (Dates and deadlines)
6. **Who Verified** (Signatures and verification)

---

## Additional Notes

### Asset Valuation Guidelines:
- Real property: Market value at date of death
- Securities: 75% of market price for listed securities
- Bank accounts: Face value
- Personal property: Estimated fair market value
- Insurance: Only policies without designated beneficiary

### Timeline Critical Dates:
- Bouppteckning must be completed: Within 3 months of death
- Bouppteckning must be registered with Skatteverket: Within 1 month of completion
- This timing affects estate administration, inheritance taxes, and property transfers

### Special Considerations:
- If married: Surviving spouse's assets may be included in same bouppteckning
- If complex estate: May require professional estate administrator (boutredningsman)
- If disputed: May require court involvement (domstol) for arvskifte (estate settlement)
